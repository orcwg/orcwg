## ORC WG Workshop

🗓️ **Thursday, January 30 from 10:00 to 17:00**<br>
📍 **Sparks Meeting**, 60 Rue Ravenstein, 1000 Brussels, Belgium

_Free admission, open to everyone, priority given to working group members and contributors._
