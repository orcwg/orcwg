## Scope

The Scope of the Cyber Resilience SIG is a strict subset of the scope of the working group. Whereas the working group is chartered to address any kind of emerging regulation impacting open source, this SIG is solely focused on Artificial Intelligence regulation, policy and standardisation.


## Why a  SIG?

As stated in the Vision and Scope section of the Open Regulatory Compliance Working Group Charter, the working group is chartered to “establish Special Interest Groups (SIGs) for domain-specific work.” Based on the successful implementation of the Cyber Resilience SIG and given the growing relevance of AI regulation to the open source community, it becomes necessary to launch a dedicated group to focus on those specific topics maintaining an active cooperation with the Cyber Resilience SIG already established. Following the same spirit as with the first SIG, the intention is to initially cover the AI Act and European regulations, but the ambition is to work across multiple markets with all the different regulations touching upon AI, policy and compliance.
