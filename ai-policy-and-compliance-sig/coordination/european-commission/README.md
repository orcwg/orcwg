# 🇪🇺 European Commission Consultations — Inputs & Contributions

This directory contains official submissions, position papers, and stakeholder feedback provided to various European Commission public consultations and calls for evidence. 

The purpose of this folder is to maintain a centralized, version-controlled archive of our regulatory inputs, ensuring alignment across our working groups and policy initiatives (such as AI governance, cyber resilience, and open source ecosystems).

## 📂 Folder Structure
To keep this archive clean and searchable, please use the following naming convention for subdirectories:

`YYYY-MM-consultation-short-name`

Examples:
* 2026-06-consultation-transparency-art50
