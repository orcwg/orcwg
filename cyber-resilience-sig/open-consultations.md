# Open Consultations Relevant to the Working Group

This document tracks **active consultations, calls for input, surveys and standardisation drafts** that may be relevant for the WG’s work.  
It is updated regularly and structured into the following categories:

- **Public consultations by the European Commission**
- **Standards (ETSI, CEN/CENELEC, ISO/IEC, etc.)**
- **Surveys**
- **Other inputs (white papers, community consultations, etc.)**
