### Voluntary Attestations

* Lot’s of discussion  
* One of the goals is to assure the sustainability of Open Source  
  * Some foundations may not be able to handle forwarding money to developers  
    * Taxes in many countries, agreements etc  
    * Was not the reason they originally incorporated \- their mission is to do everything but pay the maintainers\!  
* Aeva’s presentation at CRA EG  
  * Engagement with manufacturers  
  * Engagement with Market Surveillance Authorities  
* Some Foundations are not equipped for distributing the money to maintainers, so there is a risk that this creates more inequality.  This discussion should be held by the Attestations TF, some views include that attestations should create a two way road.
