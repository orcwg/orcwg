### ORC Status Update \+ CRA Expert Group meeting

- Membership \- 63 members with 2 in the signing process. Good contacts made at CES and FOSDEM, but we are aware of the timing to get manufacturers joining the WG.  
- We’d like to get feedback on the process implemented to contribute to the Open Digital Ecosystems.  
- We are streamlining the process to contribute to established assets like the FAQ.  
- Position papers will also be a key resource in 2026, we are working on the first, but it will be a very useful tool to engage with institutions.

It was shared the draft agenda for the CRA Expert Group meeting. We are expecting documents from the EC, especially those related to guidance. In any case we have already organised a WG call to work on the preparation of the meeting on the 23rd of February.  
There will be a presentation of the work done in the attestation project.  
ENISA will have a significant role in the meeting, presenting SBOM work, the reporting platform and the SME support. We will focus on the first two elements.

### 2026 Implementation Plan

It was introduced the differnt proposals for executing the concepts included in the program plan. The details are included in the slides.  
It was also introduced Marta Garcia to the team, since she will be supporting the working group in our outreach to member states and some trade associations.

### Code and Compliance FOSDEM Edition retrospective

These are the lessons learned and feedback based on the EF team perspective and the event feedback survey done during the closing of the event.

- Too many talks, it was a bit intense and more networking time would be welcome. For that we need to balance better talks, workshops and panels.  
- We sold out, and the two-tier ticket worked pretty well; we need to adjust it for the next time the number of lower-tier tickets.  
- It will be very helpful to have a program committee to support on focusing the event.

In the open discussion, the plan for future Code\&Compliance and the audience we are trying to reach. Currently, it is our community event, and we need to keep it as such. We will be developing other events to reach out to other industries and different types of companies. Those will help us to increase our outreach. 

The most important aspect is to keep it relevant for our community and a tool to communicate our work and gather more organisations that may join the working group.
